How to Use

Drum Pads

  • Tap any pad: Play that instrument's sound
  • Multi-touch: Play multiple instruments simultaneously
  • Motion sensing: Shake or move device for stronger hits

Instruments

  • Kick: Deep bass drum sound
  • Snare: Snappy snare drum
  • Hi-Hat: Metallic hi-hat cymbal
  • Tom: Mid-range tom drum
  • Cymbal: Crash cymbal
  • Clap: Hand clap sound
  • Cowbell: Classic cowbell
  • Rim: Rim shot
  • Shaker: Rhythmic shaker sound

Effects Modifiers

  • Reverb (0-10): Adds echo/room effect to sounds
  • Distortion (0-10): Adds gritty distortion
  • Pitch (-10 to +10): Adjust pitch level (each step is one semitone)

Tips

  • Each pad plays its own sound - play multiple at once!
  • Combine multiple modifiers for unique sounds
  • Shake the device while tapping for louder, more intense hits
  • Change themes using the colored circle in the corner

Recording Loops

  • Record: Tap to start recording your beat
  • Save: Save your recorded beat to the list
  • Save & Play: Save and immediately play your beat
  • Repeat: Toggle looping for each saved beat

  • Sample: Tap to start recording audio from your microphone
