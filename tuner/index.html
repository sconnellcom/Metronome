How to Use

Getting Started

  • Start/Stop: Click "Start Tuner" to begin listening
  • Select Instrument: Choose your instrument from the dropdown
  • Play a Note: Play a string on your instrument near your device
  • Watch the Needle: Tune until the needle is centered

Understanding the Display

  • Note Name: Shows the closest detected note (e.g., E, A, D)
  • Needle Gauge: Shows if you're flat (left) or sharp (right)
  • Cents: Shows how many cents off from perfect pitch (-50 to +50)
  • Frequency: Shows the exact frequency being detected

Color Indicators

  • Green: In tune! (within ¬±5 cents)
  • Yellow: Close (within ¬±15 cents)
  • Red: Out of tune (more than 15 cents off)

Supported Instruments

  • Guitar: Standard (EADGBE), Drop D (DADGBE)
  • Bass: 4-string (EADG), 5-string (BEADG)
  • Ukulele: Standard (GCEA)
  • Violin: GDAE
  • Viola: CGDA
  • Cello: CGDA (octave lower than viola)
  • Chromatic: All notes, no specific tuning

Tips for Best Results

  • Tune in a quiet environment
  • Hold your instrument close to your device's microphone
  • Pluck strings clearly and let them ring
  • Adjust the A4 reference pitch if needed (default: 440 Hz)
